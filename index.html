About Techfestival
September 5-10, 2017: More than 16,000 humans gathered in Copenhagen to explore the impact of technology on our lives. Over the course of six days 132 events took place in & around Kødbyen, Copenhagen’s iconic Meatpacking District. Together with people of all ages, backgrounds and nationalities we started a new conversation on the relationship between humans & technology.
